About Horizon Learning

Horizon Learning connects students and teachers through AI, enhancing learning experiences and expanding education for all.

Our platform provides personalized academic assistance—from videos to notes—and allows teachers to efficiently manage and curate class materials. We are more than an education platform; we are a community of learners.

Personalization

Our Journey

Founded in 2024 by three Annie Wright students passionate about entrepreneurship, education, and technology, Horizon Learning is currently advancing through its development stage. By the end of 2024, we will launch at various educational institutions across Western Washington, connecting with investors and expanding our reach to revolutionize education as we know it.
